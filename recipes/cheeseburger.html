Cheese Burger

Description

The cheeseburger is an American staple. A juicy beef patty between a bun

with cheese, pickles, onions, and ketchup. It doesn't get much better than that.

Ingredients:

  • Beef Patty
  • Cheese
  • Potato Bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Bacon
  • Pickels

Steps

  1. Cook patty medium rare with a red center
  2. Top patty with cheese when there's about one minute left of cooking
  3. Transfer patty to bun, top with ketchup, mustard, cooked bacon, and a pickel or two
